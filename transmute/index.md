#### Decentralized Identifiers

##### [View Source on GitHub](https://github.com/transmute-industries/ns.did.ai)

##### v1

This context strives for pragmatic extension of [https://www.w3.org/ns/did/v1](https://www.w3.org/ns/did/v1) to support [Credentials v1](https://www.w3.org/2018/credentials/v1) and [Security v2](https://w3id.org/security/v2).

We have dropped rarely used or unsafe terms.
